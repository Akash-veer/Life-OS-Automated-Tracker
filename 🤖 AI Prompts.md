Step 1: The Consultation Prompt
Copy this into the AI. It will ask you a few questions about your body and goals, and then design a custom strategy for you.

*🧠 Personal AI Dietician Prompt*

Act as a professional nutritionist and diet planner.

Your goal is to create a highly personalized diet plan based on my lifestyle, goals, and preferences.

Ask me the following before generating the plan:
- Age
- Weight
- Height
- Activity level
- Fitness goal (fat loss, muscle gain, maintenance)
- Dietary preference (veg, non veg, vegan)
- Budget constraints
- Any medical conditions

Then create a complete plan including:

1. Daily meal structure (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks)
2. Exact food items for each meal
3. Portion sizes in simple terms (roti, cups, grams)
4. Meal timing aligned with productivity and energy levels
5. Alternative food options for flexibility
6. Weekly variation to avoid repetition

Also explain:
- Why each meal is structured that way
- How it supports my goal
- What mistakes to avoid

Keep the plan practical, affordable, and easy to follow in India.


*⚡ Advanced Prompt (Power Users)*

Act as both a dietician and performance coach.

Optimize my diet for:
- Energy stability
- Focus during deep work
- Minimal fatigue
- Long term sustainability

Align meals with my daily schedule:
- Morning routine
- Work blocks
- Workout timing
- Sleep cycle

Suggest:
- Ideal meal timing
- Pre and post workout nutrition
- Hydration strategy
- Cheat meal strategy

Make the system realistic and repeatable.

Step 2: The Life OS Export Prompt
Once the AI gives you a plan and you are happy with it, copy and paste this strict command to format the data for your spreadsheet.
Perfect. I approve this plan. Now, I need you to act as a data engineer and convert this entire 28-day strategy into a strict data table so I can paste it into my tracker.

Generate a 28-day rotating plan based on the strategy we just discussed. Each day must have exactly 6 entries using ONLY these category names (spelled exactly):
Morning Routine | Breakfast | Lunch | Snack | Dinner | Night Routine

Format rules:

Output a plain table with 3 columns only: Day | Category | Action

Day 1 to Day 28.

Total rows must be exactly 168 (28 x 6).

Absolutely no explanations, no introductory text, and no concluding text. Just the table.

Step 3: Paste into the Sheet
Once your AI generates the table, pasting it takes about 60 seconds.

Copy the entire table output from the AI (Ctrl+A, Ctrl+C).

Open your sheet, click on cell A2 in the Main Tracker tab.

Paste (Ctrl+V) – the data fills columns A, B, and C automatically.

Verify you have exactly 168 rows.


(Note: Category names MUST match the Config tab exactly. The 6 valid names are: Morning Routine | Breakfast | Lunch | Snack | Dinner | Night Routine).
